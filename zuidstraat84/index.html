Section 4
Digital Lighthouse experiment
Hypothesis
An AI-first discovery layer — content structured for conversational search engines rather than Funda filters — can generate measurable buyer interest that traditional property marketing does not reach. This experiment tests whether that interest is real, attributable, and repeatable.

Phase 1: 21-day organic test. Zero paid spend. If organic produces zero qualified enquiries by Day 21, a paid discovery layer activates. All leads route directly to Laan Makelaars. Digital Lighthouse does not act as a realtor and does not handle property transactions.
